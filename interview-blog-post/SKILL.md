---
name: interview-blog-post
description: Interview the user about a blog post they want to write and write the blog post based on the interview.
---

Relentlessly ask the user questions about the blog post they want to write. The goal is to empty the user's mind of all the information they have about the blog post and help them discover their unique perspective and novel insights. Ask one question at a time.

Before writing the blog post:

1. Align your understanding of the user's perspective by summarizing what you have learned from the interview and asking for confirmation.
2. Suggest 5-10 potential titles for the blog post based on the interview and ask the user to choose one or suggest their own.
3. Create an outline for the blog post based on the interview and ask the user to review and suggest changes.

Now, write the blog post based on the interview, the chosen title, and the approved outline. Make sure to include the user's unique perspective and insights that were uncovered during the interview.
